The asymmetry: The tradition splits Viṣṇu's attributes maximally (9/9). It will NOT split Mahādeva's name. That asymmetry IS the sectarian theology: Viṣṇu's attributes are enumerable. Śiva's name is indivisible.

71 characters of Sanskrit.
46 places where the string can be cut.
271.8 billion valid ways to read it.

The tradition uses 8 cuts.
It ignores 38.

Among those 38: the split that breaks Mahādeva
into Mahā + Deva.

The grammar permits it.
The theology forbids it.
That is the finding.
